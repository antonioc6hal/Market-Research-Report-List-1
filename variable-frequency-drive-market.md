<p><p>The variable-frequency drive (VFD) market is categorized into low voltage, medium voltage, and high voltage segments. Low voltage VFDs (up to 1 kV) are widely used in small applications like fans and pumps, offering cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. Medium voltage VFDs (1 kV to 5 kV) cater to industrial applications, balancing efficiency and power management. High voltage VFDs (above 5 kV) are designed for large-scale operations, such as mining and power plants, where handling significant power loads is essential for optimal performance.</p></p>
