<p><ul><li>Conduction Pump</li><li>Induction Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electromagnetic pumps for liquid metals consist mainly of two types: conduction pumps and induction pumps. Conduction pumps utilize an electrical current to generate a magnetic field that drives the liquid metal flow, effectively allowing for precise control without moving parts. In contrast, induction pumps employ a rotating magnetic field to induce motion in the liquid metal, enabling efficient propulsion. Both types are vital for applications in engineering and energy systems, offering unique advantages in handling molten metals and high-temperature applications.</p></p>
