<p><p>The Fully Automatic Defibrillators market comprises three main types: Automatic External Defibrillators (AEDs), Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs), and others. AEDs are portable devices designed for laypersons to use during cardiac emergencies, delivering shock to restore normal heart rhythm. ICDs, on the other hand, are surgically implanted devices that continuously monitor heart activity and automatically deliver shocks when life-threatening arrhythmias are detected. The "Others" category includes specialized defibrillation devices used in specific medical contexts, enhancing overall cardiac care.</p></p>
