<p><p>IC packaging substrates are crucial for connecting integrated circuits to circuit boards. The market can be categorized into three types: Organic substrates, which are primarily made from resin-based materials providing flexibility and high-density interconnects; Inorganic substrates, typically made from ceramic materials, offering superior thermal and electrical performance; and Composite substrates, which combine both organic and inorganic materials to leverage the advantages of each, optimizing performance, durability, and cost-effectiveness for advanced semiconductor packaging applications.</p></p>
