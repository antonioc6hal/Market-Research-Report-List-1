<p><p>Dental phosphor screen scanners are imaging devices used in dentistry to capture high-quality radiographic images. These scanners utilize phosphor plates that contain a photostimulable material, which absorbs X-ray energy and emits light when stimulated by a laser. This technology offers a digital alternative to traditional film-based radiography, providing advantages such as enhanced image quality, reduced radiation exposure, and improved workflow efficiency in dental practices.</p><p>The Dental Phosphor Screen Scanners Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.90% during the forecast period. <p><strong>The Dental Phosphor Screen Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental phosphor screen scanners are categorized into two main types: conventional and digital. Conventional types utilize photostimulable phosphor plates to capture dental images, which are then read by a scanner that converts them to digital format. In contrast, digital types employ direct sensor technology, capturing images instantly in a digital format without the need for intermediate steps. Both types offer unique advantages in diagnostic imaging, influencing their adoption in dental practices based on factors like cost, efficiency, and image quality.</p></p>
