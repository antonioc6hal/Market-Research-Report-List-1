LGA packages provide a robust connection with multiple pins for high-density applications, ensuring better thermal performance. mPCIe packages offer compactness and flexibility, making them ideal for space-limited automotive environments. The "Others" category includes various alternative packaging solutions that cater to specific automotive requirements, enhancing connectivity and functionality for IoT applications in vehicles.</p></p>
