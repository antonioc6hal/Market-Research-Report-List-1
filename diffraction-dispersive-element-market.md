<p><p>The diffraction dispersive element market is primarily categorized into two types: ruled dispersive elements and holographic dispersive elements. Ruled dispersive elements utilize finely etched grooves to diffract light, providing precision in various optical applications. In contrast, holographic dispersive elements are created using interference patterns, offering greater flexibility and efficiency in light dispersion. Both types are essential in spectroscopy, telecommunications, and various imaging systems, enabling enhanced light manipulation and analysis across diverse industries.</p></p>
