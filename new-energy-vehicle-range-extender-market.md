<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) range extender market includes two main types: water-cooled and air-cooled range extenders. Water-cooled range extenders use a liquid cooling system to efficiently dissipate heat, offering better thermal management and potentially higher performance. In contrast, air-cooled range extenders rely on ambient air for cooling, typically resulting in a simpler design and reduced weight. Both types aim to enhance the driving range of electric vehicles, addressing range anxiety and improving overall user experience in the NEV sector.</p></p>
