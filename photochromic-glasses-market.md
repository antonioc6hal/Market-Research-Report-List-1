<p><strong>The Photochromic Glasses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Substrate Siscoloration Glasses</li><li>Film-Changing Glasses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photochromic glasses market encompasses two main types: substrate discoloration glasses and film-changing glasses. Substrate discoloration glasses undergo a chemical transformation within the lens material when exposed to UV light, darkening to reduce glare and enhance visibility. On the other hand, film-changing glasses utilize a thin photochromic film applied to a stable substrate, allowing the lenses to adjust tint based on light exposure. Both types provide eye protection and comfort, catering to diverse consumer needs in various lighting conditions.</p></p>
