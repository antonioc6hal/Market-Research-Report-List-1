<p><ul><li>Primary Packaging</li><li>Secondary Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The healthcare packaging market is divided into primary and secondary packaging types. Primary packaging involves the direct containment of medications or medical devices, ensuring their safety, sterility, and efficacy. It includes vials, blisters, and syringes. Secondary packaging refers to the outer packaging that groups primary packages together for distribution and protection, often featuring branding and information. This includes boxes and cartons designed for retail display or shipment, ensuring products remain safe throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
