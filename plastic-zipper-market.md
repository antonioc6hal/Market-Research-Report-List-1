<p><p>The plastic zipper market comprises three main types: close-end zippers, open-end zippers, and two-way zippers. Close-end zippers are designed for applications where the zipper does not need to separate completely, commonly used in bags and jackets. Open-end zippers allow full separation, ideal for items like jackets and sleeping bags. Two-way zippers enable access from both ends, suitable for larger garments and bags, enhancing versatility. Each type caters to specific functional needs within the garment and accessory industries.</p></p>
