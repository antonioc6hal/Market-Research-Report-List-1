<p><p>Waterproof breathable textiles are designed to repel water while allowing moisture vapor to escape, ensuring comfort. The market includes three main types: Densely Woven fabrics use tightly woven fibers to block water and enable breathability. Coated textiles apply a polymer layer for waterproofing, with micro-porous or hydrophilic coatings for breathability. Membrane textiles incorporate a thin, permeable layer laminated to the fabric, offering high waterproofing and breathability. Each type caters to diverse applications, balancing durability, performance, and cost for outdoor, sportswear, and industrial uses.</p></p>
