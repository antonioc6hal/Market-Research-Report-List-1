<p><p>The Water Knife Cutting Machine market is primarily categorized into two types: Sand Water Knife and Pure Water Knife. Sand Water Knives utilize a mixture of water and abrasive materials, providing precision cutting for harder materials like metal and stone. They are ideal for applications requiring high cutting speed and efficiency. In contrast, Pure Water Knives rely solely on high-pressure water streams, making them suitable for softer materials such as food and delicate fabrics, ensuring clean cuts without contamination.</p></p>
