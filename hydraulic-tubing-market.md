<p><strong>The Hydraulic Tubing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spiral Wire Hydraulic Tubing</li><li>Wire Braided Hydraulic Tubing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic tubing market comprises primarily two types: spiral wire hydraulic tubing and wire braided hydraulic tubing. Spiral wire hydraulic tubing features multiple layers of steel wires wound in a spiral pattern, offering excellent flexibility and high burst pressure resistance, making it suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, wire braided hydraulic tubing incorporates braided wire layers around the rubber hose, providing strength and support while maintaining flexibility. Both types serve critical functions in various hydraulic systems across industries, ensuring efficient fluid transfer and operational safety.</p></p>
