<p><p>The abdominal stent graft market can be classified into two main types: integrated and split. Integrated stent grafts combine the stent and graft components into a single, unified product, offering simplicity and ease of deployment. In contrast, split stent grafts consist of separate stent and graft components, allowing for more customization and potentially better adaptability to various anatomical conditions. Both types aim to treat abdominal aortic aneurysms effectively, but their designs cater to different surgical preferences and patient needs.</p></p>
