<p><p>Melamine Moulding Compounds (MMCs) are versatile thermosetting plastics used in various applications due to their durability and heat resistance. The two primary market types are injection molding and compression molding. Injection molding involves forcing molten MMC into a mold to create complex shapes, ideal for high-volume production. Compression molding, on the other hand, involves placing MMC into a heated mold and applying pressure, suitable for larger parts and lower production runs. Both methods capitalize on the unique properties of melamine, enhancing product performance.</p></p>
