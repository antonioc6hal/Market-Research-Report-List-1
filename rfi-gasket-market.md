<p><ul><li>Shielding Gaskets</li><li>Conductive Gaskets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFI gasket market is primarily divided into two key types: shielding gaskets and conductive gaskets. Shielding gaskets provide electromagnetic interference (EMI) protection by blocking unwanted frequencies, ensuring device reliability and performance. Conductive gaskets, on the other hand, facilitate electrical conductivity between surfaces, maintaining grounding and reducing signal loss. Both types play crucial roles in various applications, including telecommunications, aerospace, and consumer electronics, where protection against electromagnetic interference and ensuring electrical connections are critical for device functionality.</p></p>
