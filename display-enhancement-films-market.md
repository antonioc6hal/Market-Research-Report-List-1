<p><strong>The Display Enhancement Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Enhancement</li><li>Active Enhancement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Display Enhancement Films market is categorized into two main types: passive enhancement and active enhancement. Passive enhancement films improve visual performance through optical properties like light diffusion and reflection, enhancing brightness and color clarity without additional energy. In contrast, active enhancement films utilize technologies such as liquid crystals or electrochromic elements to dynamically adjust display characteristics based on conditions, providing a more interactive and adaptable visual experience. Both types aim to optimize display quality in various applications.</p></p>
