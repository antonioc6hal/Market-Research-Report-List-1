<p><p>The industrial pearlescent pigment market is categorized into natural mica-based and synthetic mica-based types. Natural mica-based pigments are derived from natural mineral sources and offer unique luster and optical effects, making them popular in cosmetics, automotive, and coatings applications. In contrast, synthetic mica-based pigments are artificially produced, allowing for greater consistency and purity in color and texture. Both types are valued for their ability to enhance products with a shimmering appearance, but they differ in sourcing, manufacturing processes, and various applications.</p></p>
