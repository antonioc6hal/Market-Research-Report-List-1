Febuxostat, a selective xanthine oxidase inhibitor, reduces uric acid production. Benzbromarone enhances uric acid excretion via the kidneys. Allopurinol, another xanthine oxidase inhibitor, is widely used to lower uric acid levels. Colchicine alleviates pain and inflammation during acute gout attacks by disrupting the inflammatory response. Together, these medications cater to diverse patient needs and contribute to the overall management of gout.</p></p>
