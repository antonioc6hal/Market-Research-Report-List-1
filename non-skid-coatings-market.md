<p><p>The Non-Skid Coatings market is primarily segmented into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based coatings utilize water as a primary solvent, offering lower VOC emissions and easier cleanup, making them environmentally friendly. They are suitable for indoor applications. Solvent-based coatings, on the other hand, use organic solvents, providing stronger adhesion and durability, ideal for harsh outdoor conditions. Each type caters to varying needs, balancing performance, environmental considerations, and application suitability across diverse surfaces and industries.</p></p>
