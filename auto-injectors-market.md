<p><p>The auto injectors market comprises four main types: SFI (Single Fuel Injection), GDI (Gasoline Direct Injection), SFI+GDI (combining both SFI and GDI technologies), and DDI (Diesel Direct Injection). SFI systems typically offer a simpler injection method for gasoline engines, while GDI provides enhanced efficiency and performance through direct fuel injection. SFI+GDI combines advantages of both technologies, increasing versatility. DDI focuses on diesel engines, delivering improved fuel efficiency and emission control, catering to diverse automotive needs and regulatory standards.</p></p>
