<p><strong>The 3D Fabrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Woven</li><li>Orthogonal Nonwoven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D fabrics market includes woven, orthogonal, and nonwoven types, each with distinct characteristics. Woven 3D fabrics are created by interlacing yarns in multiple directions, offering high strength and flexibility, ideal for composites and aerospace. Orthogonal 3D fabrics feature yarns aligned in three perpendicular directions, providing superior structural integrity for high-performance applications. Nonwoven 3D fabrics are bonded fibers without weaving, offering lightweight, cost-effective solutions for filtration and insulation. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, balancing strength, durability, and functionality.</p></p>
