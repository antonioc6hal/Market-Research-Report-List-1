<p><p>The Automotive Air Cleaner Element market includes two main types: Dry Type and Wet Type. Wet Type elements, often made of foam or oil-bath filters, capture contaminants through oil-coated surfaces, providing better performance in dusty environments but requiring regular cleaning and oiling. Both types are essential for maintaining engine health by ensuring clean air intake, with Dry Type being more common in modern vehicles.</p></p>
