<p><p>CMMS (Computerized Maintenance Management System) software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based CMMS software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access it via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premises CMMS software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Both types aim to streamline maintenance operations and improve asset management efficiency.</p></p>
