<p><ul><li>Hammer Forging Die</li><li>Hot Extrusion Die</li><li>Die casting Die</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hot-Work Die Steels market comprises three primary types: Hammer Forging Dies, Hot Extrusion Dies, and Die Casting Dies. Hammer Forging Dies are designed for high-impact applications, facilitating the shaping of metal through hammering. Hot Extrusion Dies enable the continuous shaping of materials by forcing heated metal through a die opening. Die Casting Dies are used for producing complex shapes with precision by injecting molten metal into a mold. Each type serves distinct manufacturing needs in industries like automotive and aerospace.</p></p>
