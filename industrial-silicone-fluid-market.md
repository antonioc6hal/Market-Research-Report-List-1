<p><ul><li>Straight Silicone Fluid</li><li>Modified Silicone Fluid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial silicone fluid market is primarily categorized into two types: straight silicone fluid and modified silicone fluid. Straight silicone fluid consists of linear or cyclic siloxane chains, offering high thermal stability and excellent lubricating properties, making it ideal for various industrial applications. Modified silicone fluid, on the other hand, incorporates additives or structural changes to enhance specific properties, such as improved shear strength or reduced surface tension, catering to diverse applications in coatings, textiles, and personal care products.</p></p>
