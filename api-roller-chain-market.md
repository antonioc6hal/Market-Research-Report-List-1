<p><p>The API roller chain market is divided into two main types: single strand and multi-strand chains. Single strand chains are typically used for lighter applications, featuring a simple design that makes them cost-effective and easy to maintain. In contrast, multi-strand chains consist of multiple strands, providing higher strength and load capacity, suitable for heavy-duty applications. The choice between single and multi-strand chains often depends on the specific requirements of the machinery, including the load, speed, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
