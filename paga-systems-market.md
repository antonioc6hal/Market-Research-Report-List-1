<p><p>PA/GA systems are divided into two main market types: Traditional Pressure Broadcasting and Network Broadcasting Systems. Traditional Pressure Broadcasting uses centralized amplifiers to distribute audio signals, often relying on physical cabling for transmission, making it suitable for localized applications. In contrast, Network Broadcasting Systems leverage digital networks for audio distribution, enabling real-time communication across vast areas. This modern approach allows for greater scalability, flexibility, and integration with existing IT infrastructure, catering to both large-scale and complex broadcasting needs.</p></p>
