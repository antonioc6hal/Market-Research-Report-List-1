<p><strong>The Industrial Drum Brakes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simplex</li><li>Duplex</li><li>Servo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial drum brakes market is categorized into three main types: Simplex, Duplex, and Servo. Simplex brakes feature a single set of shoes and are commonly used in lighter applications. Duplex brakes utilize two sets of shoes for enhanced stopping power, making them suitable for heavier loads. Servo brakes offer self-energizing capabilities, where the rotation of the drum aids the braking action, providing superior efficiency and performance in demanding industrial environments. These brake types cater to varying operational requirements.</p></p>
