<p><strong>The Automotive Die Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Work Die Steel</li><li>Hot Work Die Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive die steel market comprises two primary types: cold work die steel and hot work die steel. Cold work die steel is utilized for producing components in lower temperature environments, ideal for processes like stamping and shearing. Conversely, hot work die steel is designed for high-temperature applications such as forging and die-casting, offering enhanced resistance to thermal fatigue. Both types are essential for manufacturing durable automotive parts, influencing performance and efficiency in production processes within the automotive industry.</p></p>
