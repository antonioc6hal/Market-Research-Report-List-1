<p><p>Elastomeric couplings are flexible mechanical devices used to connect two shafts, transmitting torque while accommodating misalignment, reducing vibration, and absorbing shock. They consist of an elastomeric element, such as rubber or polyurethane, sandwiched between metal hubs, providing flexibility and damping properties. These couplings are widely used in industries like automotive, manufacturing, energy, and aerospace due to their durability, ease of installation, and maintenance-free operation.</p><p>The Elastomeric Coupling Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.4% during the forecast period.
