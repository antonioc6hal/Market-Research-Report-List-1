<p><p>The Soft Gasket Market includes two primary types: Semi-Metallic and Non-Metallic Materials. Semi-Metallic gaskets combine metals with soft fillers like graphite or PTFE, offering durability, high-temperature resistance, and flexibility, making them ideal for industrial applications. Non-Metallic gaskets, made from materials like rubber, fiber, or compressed sheets, provide excellent sealing, corrosion resistance, and cost-effectiveness, suitable for low-pressure environments. Both types cater to diverse industries, ensuring leak-proof connections and operational efficiency in varying conditions.</p></p>
