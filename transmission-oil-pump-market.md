<p><strong>The Transmission Oil Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Displacement Pump</li><li>Variable Displacement Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transmission Oil Pump market includes two main types: Fixed Displacement Pumps and Variable Displacement Pumps. Fixed Displacement Pumps deliver a constant flow rate, making them simple and cost-effective for consistent oil pressure needs. Variable Displacement Pumps adjust flow based on demand, offering greater efficiency and adaptability for varying transmission requirements. Both types are essential for maintaining lubrication and cooling in automotive transmissions, with the choice depending on performance, efficiency, and application-specific needs.</p></p>
