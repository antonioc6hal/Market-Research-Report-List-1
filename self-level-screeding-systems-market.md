Overlayment screed is applied over existing surfaces to refresh and level them. Pumped screed is delivered via pumps for efficiency and speed in large areas. Wearing screed provides a durable, wear-resistant surface, ideal for heavy foot traffic. Flowable screed offers excellent fluidity for easy application and leveling, making it suitable for intricate designs and contours.</p></p>
