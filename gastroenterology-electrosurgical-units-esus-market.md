<p><p>Gastroenterology Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) are essential tools used in minimally invasive procedures for tissue cutting and coagulation. The market is divided into three main types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, which utilize a single active electrode for cutting or coagulation; Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, where both electrodes are positioned at the surgical site, enhancing precision and safety; and Vessel Sealing Units, designed specifically for sealing blood vessels and tissues, minimizing bleeding during surgeries. Each type caters to different surgical needs and enhances procedural outcomes.</p></p>
