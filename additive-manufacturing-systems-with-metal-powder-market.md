<p><p>Additive Manufacturing Systems using metal powder encompass techniques like Selective Laser Melting (SLM), Electron Beam Melting (EBM), and others. SLM utilizes a high-powered laser to selectively fuse metal powders layer by layer, offering high precision and complex geometries. EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum to achieve similar results but is typically faster and suited for high-temperature materials. Other methods may include binder jetting or laser sintering, each catering to specific applications and material requirements in the manufacturing landscape.</p></p>
