<p><p>The trenchless machinery market includes various technologies like Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) and Pipe Jacking machines. HDD is primarily used for installing pipelines and cables beneath obstacles such as roads and rivers without disturbing the surface, offering precision and minimal environmental impact. Pipe Jacking machines, on the other hand, involve the thrust of pipes into the ground, often for sewer and utility installations, facilitating efficient excavation with reduced surface disruption. Both methods enable rapid, cost-effective infrastructure development.</p></p>
