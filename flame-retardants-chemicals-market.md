<p><p>The flame retardants chemicals market is primarily divided into two main types: non-halogenated and halogenated flame retardants. Non-halogenated flame retardants are considered more environmentally friendly, using materials like phosphorous, nitrogen, or inorganic compounds to inhibit combustion without releasing harmful gases. In contrast, halogenated flame retardants contain bromine or chlorine and are highly effective but may pose environmental and health risks due to their persistent nature. Both types are crucial in enhancing fire safety across various industries, including construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
