<p><p>Copper pipes, coils, and fittings are categorized into three main types based on wall thickness and application: Type K, L, and M. Type K pipes have the thickest walls and are ideal for underground and high-pressure applications. Type L pipes are suitable for general plumbing and HVAC systems, offering a balance of strength and weight. Type M pipes, with the thinnest walls, are often used for residential water systems and light-duty applications due to their cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
