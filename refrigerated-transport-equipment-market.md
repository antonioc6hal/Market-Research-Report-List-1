<p><strong>The Refrigerated Transport Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-blown Evaporators</li><li>Eutectic Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigerated transport equipment market includes various types such as air-blown evaporators and eutectic devices. Air-blown evaporators utilize fans to circulate cold air throughout the cargo area, ensuring uniform temperature control for perishable goods. Eutectic devices, on the other hand, use phase change materials that absorb and release thermal energy to maintain a stable temperature, making them energy-efficient alternatives. Both technologies are crucial for preserving the integrity of temperature-sensitive products during transportation, ensuring food safety and quality.</p></p>
