<p><p>Modular wall systems are categorized into three market types based on their permanence. Permanent modular walls are designed for long-term use, providing durable, fixed partitions ideal for commercial spaces. Semi-permanent systems offer flexibility, allowing for reconfiguration while maintaining a more stable structure than temporary solutions; they are suited for businesses that anticipate changes. Temporary modular walls are easily assembled and disassembled, making them perfect for short-term projects or events, offering a mobile and cost-effective partitioning solution.</p></p>
