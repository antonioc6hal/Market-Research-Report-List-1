Horizontal sterilizers feature a cylindrical design that allows for efficient heat transfer and processing of large volumes of dairy products. Vertical types utilize a stacked arrangement, optimizing space and facilitating easy maintenance. Spiral sterilizers, designed in a continuous loop, enable high throughput and reduced footprint, making them ideal for large-scale operations. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to various production requirements in the dairy industry.</p></p>
