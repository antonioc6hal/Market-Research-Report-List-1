<p><p>Internal broaching machines are classified into vertical and horizontal types based on their design and operation. Vertical broaching machines have a vertically oriented tool path, suitable for producing deep internal features with precision and efficiency. In contrast, horizontal broaching machines operate with a horizontal tool path, ideal for broader, shallower cuts and larger components. Each type caters to specific manufacturing needs, offering flexibility and efficiency for shaping internal geometries in various materials across different industries.</p></p>
