<p><strong>The PECVD Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RF-PECVD</li><li>ECR-PECVD</li><li>MW-PECVD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PECVD (Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition) systems are categorized into three main types based on the excitation source: RF-PECVD, ECR-PECVD, and MW-PECVD. RF-PECVD utilizes radio frequency power to generate plasma, suitable for various films. ECR-PECVD employs Electron Cyclotron Resonance for better plasma density and uniformity, ideal for advanced materials. MW-PECVD utilizes microwave frequency for high-quality films with lower substrate heating, catering to industries requiring precise deposition. Each type serves distinct applications in semiconductor and thin-film technology.</p></p>
