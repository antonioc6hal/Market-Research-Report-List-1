<p><p>Flame-resistant (FR) fabrics are designed to resist ignition and self-extinguish when exposed to flames. **Inherent FR fabrics** are made from fibers with built-in flame-resistant properties, offering durable protection even after repeated washing. **Treated FR fabrics** are chemically treated to achieve flame resistance, but their effectiveness may diminish over time with wear and washing. **Hybrid FR fabrics** combine inherent and treated fibers, balancing durability and cost-effectiveness. Each type serves industries like oil & gas, utilities, and military, ensuring worker safety in high-risk environments.</p></p>
