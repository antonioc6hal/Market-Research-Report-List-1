<p><p>Transmissive Spatial Light Modulators (SLMs) are classified into two main types: Amplitude Type and Amplitude and Phase Type. Amplitude Type SLMs control light intensity by varying transmission, suitable for applications like image projection and displays. In contrast, Amplitude and Phase Type SLMs manipulate both light intensity and phase, enabling advanced functionalities like holography and optical beam shaping. These technologies are crucial in fields such as telecommunications, medical imaging, and adaptive optics, driving innovations in optical processing and display systems.</p></p>
