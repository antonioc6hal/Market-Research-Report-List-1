<p><p>The handheld infrared thermometer market comprises various types, primarily the forehead and ear thermometers. Forehead thermometers use infrared technology to measure surface temperature quickly and non-invasively, ideal for general health assessments, especially in children. In contrast, ear thermometers measure temperature via the tympanic membrane and provide accurate readings rapidly, making them suitable for adults and accurate diagnostics. Both types offer convenience and speed, catering to home healthcare, clinical settings, and pandemic-related needs, enhancing overall monitoring efficiency.</p></p>
