<p><p>The gas and particle sensors market primarily consists of two types: gas sensors and particle sensors. Gas sensors detect the presence and concentration of various gases, including toxic, flammable, and greenhouse gases, utilizing technologies like electrochemical, infrared, and semiconductor methods. Particle sensors, on the other hand, monitor airborne particulate matter and pollutants to assess air quality, employing methods such as laser scattering and light obscuration. Both types are crucial for environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and health applications.</p></p>
