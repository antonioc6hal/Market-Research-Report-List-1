<p><p>The outdoor solar panels market is categorized into foldable and non-foldable solar panels. Foldable solar panels are lightweight, portable, and designed for easy transport, making them ideal for camping and outdoor activities. They can be set up quickly and are convenient for users on the go. In contrast, non-foldable solar panels are typically larger and fixed installations, suited for residential and commercial use. They offer higher energy output and durability for long-term energy solutions but lack the mobility of their foldable counterparts.</p></p>
