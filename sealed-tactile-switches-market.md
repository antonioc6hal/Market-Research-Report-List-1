<p><p>The sealed tactile switches market comprises two main types: surface-mounting and through-hole mounting. Surface-mounting switches are designed for easy assembly on the surface of printed circuit boards (PCBs), allowing for a compact design and efficient space utilization. In contrast, through-hole mounting switches involve inserting leads through holes in the PCB, providing robust mechanical support and better durability. Both types are essential for various applications across consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial devices where reliable tactile feedback is crucial.</p></p>
