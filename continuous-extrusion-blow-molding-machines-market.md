<p><ul><li>Shuttle Machines</li><li>Rotary Wheel Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous extrusion blow molding machines are categorized into shuttle machines and rotary wheel machines. Shuttle machines operate by moving a mold on a shuttle system, allowing for efficient production and quick cycle times, ideal for large parts or varied product sizes. Rotary wheel machines, on the other hand, utilize a rotating table with multiple molds to optimize production speed and efficiency, making them suitable for high-volume manufacturing. Both types offer enhanced performance and flexibility in producing hollow plastic containers.</p></p>
