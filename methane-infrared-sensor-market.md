<p><p>The methane infrared sensor market comprises several types, primarily including scattering type and light absorption type sensors. Scattering type sensors detect methane by measuring changes in light scattered by airborne particles, while light absorption type sensors identify methane by analyzing the light absorbed by the gas at specific infrared wavelengths. Other types may include sensor technologies such as photoacoustic sensors or tunable diode laser absorption spectroscopy (TDLAS) sensors. Each type offers unique advantages for methane detection in various applications, including industrial, environmental, and safety monitoring.</p></p>
