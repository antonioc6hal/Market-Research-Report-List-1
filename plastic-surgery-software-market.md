<p><p>The plastic surgery software market is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and easy access from various devices, ideal for busy practices. It often features automatic updates and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premise software is installed locally on healthcare providers' servers, requiring significant initial investment and maintenance. While it offers greater control and customization, it may limit accessibility and scalability compared to cloud options.</p></p>
