<p><p>Digital Rights Management (DRM) software can be categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise DRM solutions are installed and managed within a company's own infrastructure, providing complete control over assets but requiring significant IT resources and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based DRM offers a more flexible solution hosted on third-party servers, allowing for easy scalability and remote access while reducing the need for in-house IT support. Both options aim to protect digital content from unauthorized use.</p></p>
