<p><p>Structural sealants are high-performance adhesives used to bond and seal materials in construction and industrial applications, providing strength, durability, and weather resistance. PS sealants provide strong adhesion and chemical resistance, often used in construction joints. PU sealants are durable and elastic, suitable for automotive and industrial uses.
