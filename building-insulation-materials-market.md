<p><p>The building insulation materials market comprises various types including foams, panels, glass, fiber, aerogels, and others. Foams, typically made from polyurethane or polystyrene, provide effective thermal resistance. Panels, such as polyisocyanurate or mineral wool, are rigid and often used in wall systems. Glass insulation involves fiberglass products known for their lightweight and non-combustible properties. Fiber insulation includes natural options like cellulose. Aerogels are advanced materials known for their exceptional insulation performance, while others encompass less common alternatives like reflective barriers and organic insulators.</p></p>
